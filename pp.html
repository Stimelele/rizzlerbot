Privacy Policy

How Rizzler handles your data and protects your privacy.

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Rizzler's Privacy Policy. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Discord bot. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Rizzler is a fun Discord bot designed for entertainment purposes. We take your privacy seriously and only collect minimal data necessary for the bot to function properly.

2. Information We Collect

Rizzler collects the following types of information:

  • Discord User Information: Your Discord user ID, username, and discriminator when you interact with the bot.
  • Server Information: Server ID and basic server metadata when Rizzler is added to a Discord server.
  • Command Usage: Commands you use and their parameters (e.g., when you use the /pull command).
  • Game Data: Your rizz pull results, collected items, and game progress.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the bot's functionality
  • To store your game progress and rizz collection
  • To improve the bot's features and user experience
  • To prevent abuse and ensure fair usage
  • For statistical analysis (anonymized and aggregated)

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and protected with the following measures:

  • Data is stored in encrypted databases
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to data (only essential personnel)
  • Regular backups to prevent data loss

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the bot to function. You can request data deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. Third-Party Services

Rizzler uses the following third-party services:

  • Discord API: For bot functionality and integration
  • Hosting Services: For bot hosting and data storage
  • Analytics Services: For usage statistics (anonymous)

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your stored data
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Opt-out: Stop using the bot at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us through our support channels.

7. Children's Privacy

Rizzler is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by:

  • Posting an announcement in our Discord server
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date on this page
  • Adding a notification in the bot's help command

Your continued use of Rizzler after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Discord Server: Join our support server
  • Email: privacy@rizzlerbot.com
  • GitHub: Open an issue on our repository
